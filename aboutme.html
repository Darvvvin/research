About Me

I am involved and one of the founding members of Developer's Society, a Zobel based tech org that promotes STEM and the development of computer science related skills such as media and code. Every so often I enjoy taking time off of school work and fancy a life apart from academics. I enjoy challenges and learning new things. Life is short, always aim for something better. Hardship often prepares an ordinary person for an extraordinary destiny.

Learning Objectives

The objective of this research was to help me improve my research and writing skills. This helps me prepare and perform better in college and become competitive in a proffesional world. My previos researches were aimed to help me and my groupmates understand the basics of writing a thesis and the principles, disciplines and etiquette of performing a study.

In general, this research course significantly helped me get a better understanding of what its like to perform a research study and how I can develop and improve my writing, surveying and research related skills.
