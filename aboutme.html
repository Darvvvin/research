About Me


Hi! I'm Jacob Darvin

I study in De La Salle Santiago Zobel under the STEM strand. I am a researcher and research conductor who has so far helped developed 4 research papers over the past 2 years of Senior Highschool. I am involved and one of the founding members of Developer's Society, a Zobel based tech org that promotes STEM and the development of computer science related skills such as media and code. Being a part of that org helped me learn a lot of skills that chanelled onto the development of my research paper. Such skills include Photoshop, HTML, CSS and many more that I use to develop most of my group's media related outputs such as powerpoints and posters.

Every so often I enjoy taking time off of school work and enjoy a life without academics.
